About Me

I am Jackie Lee, a former educator and former Java coding bootcamp student at Tech Elevator. Creating my classroom website sparked my interest in web development and coding, and led me to enroll in Tech Elevator. My goal is to use the skills I have acquired from Tech Elevator to become a Computer Programmer and to continue to challenge myself.
